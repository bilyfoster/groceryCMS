## About Page (https://www.brazentherapy.org/copy-of-about-2)
**Founded:** 2021 by Clarke Scott, LPC

**Mission:** We believe connection is strongest when we embrace difference. At Brazen, we meet you where you are, affirm who you are, and walk beside you as you create change, on your terms.

Brazen's team of compassionate and skilled therapists are committed to showing up not just as mental health professionals, but as advocates and safe harbors for those navigating life's challenges. We use evidence-based practices within a framework that centers client autonomy, prioritizes emotional safety, and honors each person's lived experience.

### Areas of Focus
- Anxiety
- Depression
- Trauma/PTSD
- Life Transitions
- QTPOC/LGBTQ+ Affirming Care
- Family of Origin Issues
- Grief/Loss
- Identity & Relationships (including non-monogamy, sex-positive/kink, body positivity, and internalized oppression)

### Therapeutic Techniques
- Solution Focused Based Therapy (SFBT)
- Narrative Therapy
- Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR)
- Internal Family Systems (IFS)
- Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT)
- Dialectical Behavioral Therapy (DBT)
- Somatic/Body Oriented Approaches
- Mindfulness Based Interventions
